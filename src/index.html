COLLAB
Recursive Minds X LSQ Design

Recursive Minds Studio and LSQ Design have come together to create games that are clever, cozy, and, most importantly, fun to make. We’re Katherine (the animation wizard), Olivia (the refactoring queen), and Lisa (the world-building sparkle machine)—three creators who believe in making games we love to play and loving the process just as much.
Recursive Minds, founded by Katherine and Olivia and the studio behind WORDS, thrives on smart design, retro aesthetics, and the joy of solving problems with friends. LSQ Design, led by Lisa and creator of Hungry Hippo Havoc, is all about stress-free, low-stakes games where competition doesn’t come at the cost of fun.
Together, our studios blend technical ingenuity, artistic charm, and a shared love of game design that sparks joy. Whether it’s crafting painterly worlds, refining the tiniest gameplay details, or just making each other laugh along the way, our collaboration is built on creativity, friendship, and the belief that games should always be a delight—win or lose.

SPRING SQUAD
A Cozy Frogventure

Welcome to Spring Squad, a whimsical co-op platformer where you and a friend sprout from wiggly tadpoles to fully fabulous frogs. Hop across hand-painted lilypads, collect sparkly flowers, and—most importantly—avoid stepping in suspiciously minty patches.
It’s a game about growing up (just a little), making silly faces, and helping each other across a soggy world of grass and water. Build lilypad bridges, giggle at tadpole physics, and, of course, wear a flower hat. No scores. No stress. Just good vibes, maximum ribbit, and the deeply held belief that “Eww, Mint!” is the most valid opinion in gaming.
If your idea of a good time is frog-jumping through a painterly pond while avoiding responsibility, you’re exactly where you’re supposed to be.
